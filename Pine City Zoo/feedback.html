WE`D LOVE TO HEAR FROM YOU

We value every visitor’s experience at the zoo and are always looking for ways to improve. Your feedback helps us grow, enhance our exhibits, and make each visit even more enjoyable for families, school groups, and animal lovers alike.

Whether you had a fantastic day or noticed something we could do better—from signage and services to animal encounters and dining—we want to hear from you. Every suggestion matters and is reviewed by our guest services team.

Comments can be submitted in person, through our digital form, or via the QR codes located around the zoo. Help us make the zoo better for everyone, one idea at a time.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You’ll find Feedback Stations near the main exit, inside the Restaurant, and at the Guest Services kiosk by the North Gate.
