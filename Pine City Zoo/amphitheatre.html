AMPHITHEATRE

The Amphitheatre is the heart of the zoo’s live entertainment. Designed with a natural stage and tiered seating, it offers a comfortable setting for animal demonstrations, educational talks, and interactive performances. Whether it’s learning about raptor flight patterns or enjoying a wildlife-themed puppet show, the Amphitheatre brings fun and learning together for all ages.

Shows are scheduled throughout the day and are often led by expert zookeepers and conservationists who provide unique insight into animal behavior and global conservation efforts. It’s a great place to rest your feet while feeding your curiosity.

The open-air design ensures a connection to nature, and the shaded seating areas make it a perfect pit stop between exhibits.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

The Amphitheatre is located near the center of the zoo, just opposite the Elephant enclosure at pen E22.
