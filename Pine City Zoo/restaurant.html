RESTAURANT

The zoo’s full-service Restaurant provides a wide menu for families and groups looking for a hearty meal. With indoor seating and large windows that overlook the giraffe enclosure, it combines good food with a great view.

The menu includes salads, sandwiches, burgers, pasta dishes, and a kids’ corner with animal-shaped meals. Sustainability is key here—leftover food is composted and single-use plastics are minimized.

Perfect for birthday bookings, school trips, or just a mid-day refuel, the Restaurant is the zoo’s main dining hub.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

The Restaurant is near the South Gate, close to the gift shop and first aid center.
