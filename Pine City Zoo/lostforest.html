LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a lush, winding pathway surrounded by tall trees, dense vegetation, and hidden animal sculptures. It’s a peaceful zone designed to replicate a temperate forest biome, complete with misty corners and birdsong playing through hidden speakers.

It encourages mindful exploration, perfect for visitors who want a quiet nature walk away from the more crowded animal exhibits. Interpretive signs offer forest facts and scavenger hunt clues for kids.

It’s also home to a few secret surprises—including a fairy door trail and a hidden viewpoint over the zoo’s wetlands.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You can access the Lost Forest from the west side of the zoo, behind the Amphitheatre and next to the Coffee Shop.
