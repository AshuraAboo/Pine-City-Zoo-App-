INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House offers a close-up look into the miniature world of insects. From fluttering butterflies to marching ants and glowing beetles, this climate-controlled exhibit showcases biodiversity on a tiny scale.

Educational panels, interactive magnifiers, and live demonstrations help visitors understand the vital role insects play in ecosystems—from pollination to decomposition.

Designed with dark zones, lit terrariums, and a butterfly walk-through section, it’s a favorite for young learners and budding entomologists.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

The Insect House is located near the Reptile Dome, across from the Pizza Shop.
