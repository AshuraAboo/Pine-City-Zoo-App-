COFFEE SHOP

The Coffee Shop is a quiet retreat for visitors in need of a caffeine boost or a sweet snack. Offering a selection of freshly brewed coffees, herbal teas, pastries, and light bites, it’s a popular spot for adults while kids enjoy nearby attractions.

With indoor and shaded outdoor seating, it’s designed to offer comfort in every season. The café uses eco-friendly packaging and sources local ingredients where possible, in line with the zoo’s sustainability goals.

Whether you’re warming up on a cool morning or cooling down after a trek through the zoo, the Coffee Shop is your go-to spot to relax and recharge.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You’ll find the Coffee Shop at the East Gate, near the entrance to the Lost Forest trail.
